About Washington Construction

Manuel Sarabia-Garcia, the founder of Washington Construction, has built his reputation on integrity, craftsmanship, and genuine client care. With deep roots in the Pacific Northwest, Manuel understands the specific challenges and opportunities of landscaping and construction in Pierce County. His experience spans two decades and includes everything from lawn care and hardscaping to home renovations, roofing, and large-scale concrete projects.

Washington Construction is committed to clear communication, fair pricing, and quality that lasts. Whether you're revamping a backyard or upgrading your home's exterior, Manuel and his team are ready to help you build something that stands strong and looks great.
